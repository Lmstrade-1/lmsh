Contact

Enquiries are handled by appointment only.

Initial contact is used to establish basic details and suitability before any meeting or inspection is arranged. Watches are not accepted for work without prior discussion.

Each piece passes through a distinct inspection and assessment phase. Following inspection, findings and potential approaches are discussed before any work is scheduled. As outlined elsewhere on this site, quotations are not provided prior to inspection.

Please use the contact form below to make initial contact. Clear photographs and any known information about the watch are helpful at this stage.
